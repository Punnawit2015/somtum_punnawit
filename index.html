Somtum

Somtum(ส้มตำ) is a famous Thai papaya salad know for it bold flaver

lime juice, plam sugar.Originating from northeastern region of Thailand(Isan).

Somtum comes in many viriation,including somtum Thai (sweeter and milder),

Somtum Poo Pla Ra(With fermented fish and crab),and Somtum Kai Kem (with salted egg)

The dish is commonly enjoyed with rice grilled meat,making it a staple in Thai cuisine.
